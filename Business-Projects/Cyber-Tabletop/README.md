# Cyber Tabletop Exercise Projects

This section contains **cybersecurity tabletop exercises** designed to simulate real-world cyber incidents and evaluate organizational preparedness, response coordination, and decision-making.

- Incident simulation and scenario design
- Executive and technical response planning
- Communication and escalation workflows
- Risk, compliance, and business impact analysis

## Types of Projects Included
- Ransomware response tabletop exercises
- Data breach management scenarios
- Executive decision-making simulations
- Crisis communication planning
- Post-incident review and lessons learned

## Skills Demonstrated
- Incident response coordination
- Risk and crisis management
- Stakeholder communication
- Strategic cybersecurity planning
